Power of Heroes!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. This number is the power of your enemies

There are five heroes below. By clicking on a hero you will add a specific amount of points to the power of the heroes.

You win the game by matching the power of heroes to the enemies's power, but you lose the game if the heroes's power goes above of enemies's.

The value of each hero is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each hero and the enemies's power
